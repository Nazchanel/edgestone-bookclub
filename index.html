Lower Primary has to submit a form of the title, the author, and at least one event that happened in the book. Extra points will be awarded if the child can list more than one event that happened. 

Upper Primary or Primary has to read at least 50 pages and has to list 5 events. 

Secondary needs to read at least 110 pages and need to list 20 events. 
Title, author and your name is also needed in the form.

How its going to work

7/1/2019

0 Comments

 
I want to make this post to make it clear how this is going to work. 
This book club is going to be divided into three groups: 

Lower Primary, Primary, and Secondary 
  • Upper Primary will consist of all  grades 3-5
  • Lower primary is going to be everything below that
  • Secondary is all middle and high schoolers
If any adults want to join to I will happy to invite you in 

There will be sub-categories for all these groups and they will compete. 
There will also be individual competitions too. 

I working out an incentive that  you can work toward and a prize for the competition. 
I will change this plan as we go about this.
